roulette reader

<

>

this app is meant to help you keep track of the books you'd like to read. to use it, simply type in a keyword, be it a book title, a subject, or a genre. when you click submit, you will generate a list of up to 10 books that fit your search.

each book card includes the book's cover, title, author, and genre. click "more" to read a short description of the book's content.

to choose a book and move it to your personal book roulette, simply click on the bookcover. you can cycle through the books you've already chosen by clicking on the arrows to either side of book cover.

to remove a book from your list, simply click on the cover again.
